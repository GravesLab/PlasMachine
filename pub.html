The Colbert Report

Stephen Colbert is the host, writer, and executive producer of the Emmy-winning Colbert Report. He attended the Clinton Global Initiative University 2013 meeting to interview President Bill Clinton and establish the Colbert Galactic Initiative. Colbert interviewed Matt and Connor at the CGIU Exchange and features Plasmachine on his "In the Field" website.

Clinton Global Initiative University 2013

Building on the successful model of theÂ Clinton Global Initiative, which brings together world leaders to take action on global challenges, President Clinton launched the Clinton Global Initiative University (CGI U) in 2007 to engage the next generation of leaders on college campuses around the world.

Each year, CGI U hosts a meeting where students, youth organizations, topic experts, and celebrities come together to discuss and develop innovative solutions to pressing global challenges. PlasMachine was honored as an outstanding new commitment in the area of Global Sanitation and Public Health at the April 2013 meeting.

The Daily Californian

The Daily Californian is an independent, student-run newspaper published by the Independent Berkeley Student Publishing Company, Inc. The newspaper serves the UC Berkeley campus and its surrounding community, publishing Monday through Friday during the academic year and twice a week during the summer. Established in 1871, The Daily Californian is one of the oldest newspapers on the West Coast and one of the oldest college newspapers in the country.

Blum Center for Developing Economies

The Blum Center for Developing Economies was founded in 2006 to address the problem of global poverty. Working with both the University of California and the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, the Blum Center embodies UC Berkeley's combination of global engagement and technological excellence.
